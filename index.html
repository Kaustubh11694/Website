FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to change my existing phone number?
No. You keep your existing number. We simply set up call forwarding so CONCIEIT answers on your behalf. The process takes under 5 minutes and your number stays the same.
How long does it take to get set up?
We typically have your CONCIEIT AI configured and live within 48 hours of sign-up. We handle all the setup — no technical work needed from your team.
What happens if CONCIEIT does not know the answer?
CONCIEIT is trained to gracefully handle anything it cannot answer by offering to have a team member call back. It captures the caller's name and number and sends your team an instant notification.
Can callers tell they are talking to AI?
Most callers cannot tell. Our AI voice powered by ElevenLabs sounds remarkably human — warm, natural, and professional. If a caller directly asks, CONCIEIT honestly identifies itself as a virtual concierge.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All calls are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your data or your clients' data with third parties. Our infrastructure is hosted on enterprise-grade cloud providers.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties or fees.
